## Core Implementation Modules

| Module | Full Name            | Chinese       |
| :----- | :------------------- | :------------ |
| **FI** | Financial Accounting | 财务会计      |
| **CO** | Controlling          | 管理会计/控制 |
| **AA** | Asset Accounting     | 资产管理      |
| **SD** | Sales & Distribution | 销售与分销    |
| **MM** | Material Management  | 物料管理      |
| **PP** | Production Planning  | 生产计划      |
| **QM** | Quality Management   | 质量管理      |
| **PS** | Project System       | 项目系统      |
| **PM** | Plant Maintenance    | 工厂维护      |
| **HR** | Human Resources      | 人力资源      |
| **WM** | Warehouse Management | 仓库管理      |

## Module Categories

- **Accounting**: FI, CO, AA
- **Logistics**: SD, MM, PP, PM, WM, QM
- **Supply Chain Management**
- **Human Resources**

### Cross Application Modules (跨应用模块)

- **WF**: Workflow
- **BW**: Business Warehousing (DWH)
- **CRM**: Customer Relationship Management
- **ECM**: Enterprise Content Management
- **MII**: Manufacturing Integration & Intelligence

### Technical Modules (技术性模块)

- **NetWeaver**
- **Solution Manager**
- **EP**: Enterprise Portal
- **PI**: Process Integration / PO (Process Orchestration)

### Industry Solutions (IS)

- **A**: Automotive
- **H**: Healthcare
- **M**: Media
- **PS**: Public Sector
- **R**: Retail
- **T**: Telecommunication
- **U**: Utilities
- **RE**: Real Estate
- **OIL**: Oil & Gas

### Financial Services (FS)

- **BP**: Business Partner
- **CMS**: Collateral Management
- **CML**: Consumer, Corp. Mortgage Loans
- **CM**: Claim Management
- **PM**: Policy Management
- **CD**: Collection & Disbursement
- **ICM**: Incentive / Commission Management
- **RI**: Reinsurance
