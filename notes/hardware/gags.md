# ASRock DeskMini Series Mini-PC

The ASRock DeskMini series is a line of compact, high-performance barebone mini-PCs based on the Mini-STX (5x5) form factor, which allows for replaceable desktop-grade CPUs.

## ASRock DeskMini 310 Series

A popular model designed for 8th and 9th Generation Intel Core processors.

- [DeskMini 310 Official Page (CN)](http://www.asrock.cn/nettop/Intel/DeskMini%20310%20Series/index.cn.asp)

### Key Specifications

- **CPU Support**: Supports Intel® 8th / 9th Gen Core™ / Pentium® / Celeron® LGA1151 Processors (up to 65W TDP).
- **RAM**: Dual DDR4 SO-DIMM slots, supporting up to 32GB (now 64GB with BIOS updates).
- **Video Output**: Triple output support:
  - DisplayPort (4K @ 60Hz).
  - HDMI (4K @ 30Hz).
  - D-Sub (VGA).
- **Storage**:
  - 1 x Ultra M.2 PCIe Gen3 x4 slot (2280).
  - 2 x 2.5" HDD / SSD storage trays.
  - Integrated Micro SD card reader.
- **Connectivity**:
  - 1 x M.2 (2230) Wi-Fi/BT slot.
  - USB 3.1 Gen1 Type-C port on the front panel.
  - Intel Gigabit LAN.

### Key Benefits

- **Performance**: Unlike NUCs that use mobile CPUs, the DeskMini uses standard desktop CPUs, providing significantly higher multi-core performance.
- **Expandability**: Excellent storage options for its size (M.2 + 2x 2.5" SATA).
- **Compact Form Factor**: 1.9 Liter chassis size.

## Related Models

- **DeskMini H470**: Supports Intel 10th / 11th Gen CPUs.
- **DeskMini A300 / X300**: Popular variants for AMD Ryzen APUs (AM4 socket).
- **DeskMeet Series**: Slightly larger (8L) version supporting discrete graphics cards.
