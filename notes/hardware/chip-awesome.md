# IC & Chip Resources (Awesome Chips)

Curated list of resources for Integrated Circuits (IC), chipsets, and semiconductor manufacturing.

## Databases & Wikis

- [WikiChip](https://en.wikichip.org/wiki/WikiChip) - Comprehensive knowledge base for computer chips.
- [CPUID](https://www.cpuid.com/) - Tools like CPU-Z for identifying hardware components.
- [TechPowerUp GPU Database](https://www.techpowerup.com/gpu-specs/) - Database of graphics processors.
- [AllDataSheet](https://www.alldatasheet.com/) - Search engine for electronic component datasheets.

## Major Semiconductor Manufacturers

- **Foundries (Pure Play)**:
  - **TSMC**: Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company.
  - **GlobalFoundries**: US-based foundry.
  - **SMIC**: Semiconductor Manufacturing International Corporation.
  - **Intel Foundry Services (IFS)**: Intel's foundry business.

- **Integrated Device Manufacturers (IDM)**:
  - **Intel**: x86 processors and specialty chips.
  - **Samsung Electronics**: Memory, Exynos SoCs.
  - **SK Hynix**: Memory specialist.
  - **TI (Texas Instruments)**: Analog and embedded processors.

- **Fabless Companies**:
  - **NVIDIA**: GPUs and AI accelerators.
  - **Qualcomm**: Snapdragon SoCs, communication chips.
  - **Apple**: M-series and A-series silicon.
  - **AMD**: CPUs and GPUs.
  - **MediaTek**: mobile and IoT SoCs.

## Architectures & Tech

- **Instruction Sets**: x86, ARM, RISC-V, MIPS.
- **Node Processes**: 7nm, 5nm, 3nm, GAAFET, FinFET.

## Learning Resources

- [The Art of Electronics](https://artofelectronics.net/) - Fundamental reference for IC usage.
- [Semiconductor Engineering](https://semiengineering.com/) - Industry news and deep dives.
