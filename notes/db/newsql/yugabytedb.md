- **PostgreSQL (YSQL)**
  - Compatible with PostgreSQL v11.2+
  - [Compatibility FAQ](https://docs.yugabyte.com/latest/faq/compatibility/#what-is-the-extent-of-compatibility-with-postgresql)

- **Cassandra (YCQL)**
  - Compatible with Apache Cassandra QL (CQL) v3.4
  - [Compatibility FAQ](https://docs.yugabyte.com/latest/faq/compatibility/#ycql-compatibility-with-apache-cassandra-ql)
